About me

I am Marcos David Hernández Ortigoza. An Interactive Media student who loves to program and experiment with technology. From an early age, I found a fascination at computers due to the endless possibilities of things you can do. One of the possibilities is video creation and editing, as seen in the product "The August 27th Incident".

Images

Inspiration

One of my favorite action films is called "Hardcore Henry". Directed by Ilya Naishuller and released in 2015, this movie experimented with a First Person point of view, like those seen in video games. Obviously, I do not have the expensive equipment to produce a high quality movie, but I thought: "Hey! Maybe I can do something similar with my phone camera."
